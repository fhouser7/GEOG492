Fiona Houser's Top 5 Birding Spots in Eugene, OR

This is my final project for Geography 492, Web Mapping. This map is meant to serve as an easy way for beginner birders to find a place to start. This map tells the story of the vast biodiversity that can be found in and around Eugene, and invites user's to explore for themselves.

Please take a minute to explore all the features of the map! Feel free to click on each of the five popups to learn about the species that reside in that area and what sort of environment can be found.
Featured Areas:
  • Fern Ridge Wildlife Area
  • Delta Ponds
  • Amazon Park
  • Black Oak Basin Park
  • Mt. Pitsgah Arboretum
